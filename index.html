Developing the potential of young and senior players

OPTIMUS CLASS

We aim to build our young and senior players into better footballers, by acquiring new technical, physical, psychological and social skills to reach the next stage of their development.

We will develop our players to impress scouts, coaches and academies to further their football career.

PROFESSIONAL ACADEMY UEFA COACH

Within the academy, all of our players are trained by the head coach who has experience in coaching and scouting in professional academies. All training sessions are delivered with a high intensity, professional academy standard to give our players the best teaching possible.

PROFESSIONAL
ACADEMY GAMES

We take our age groups to play professional and semi professional academy teams to give our players the best insight on what it's like at a professional standard. This develops their own individual play and also provides them with scouting opportunities.
